# TITAN LORE

The Plutonium Titan was once the leader of the ancient civilization that created plutonium technology.

During a reactor catastrophe, its body fused with an experimental reactor core.

It now exists as a massive radioactive abomination buried deep underground.

Its reactor heart continuously emits catastrophic radiation.
