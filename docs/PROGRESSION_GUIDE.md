# NuclearCraft: Plutonium Age — Progression Guide
Version 1.1.0 | Phase 13

## Overview

NuclearCraft's progression is designed as a linear but interlocking chain. Each tier of content unlocks the next. This guide explains what each stage requires, what unlocks it, and how long it's expected to take on a standard survival multiplayer server.

---

## The Full Progression Chain

```
Day 1
  ↓ Night 1 — First Irradiated Zombie
  ↓ Kill zombies — collect Radioactive Cores + Mutated Seeds
  ↓
Day 2–3
  ↓ ~20 zombie kills → enough Cores to craft Radiation Drill
  ↓
Day 4–7
  ↓ Radiation Drill → safe Plutonium Ore mining
  ↓ Mine Plutonium Ore → Raw Plutonium Fragments
  ↓
Day 7–14
  ↓ Craft Nuclear Smelter (requires Cores + vanilla materials)
  ↓ Smelt Fragments → Refined Plutonium Ingots
  ↓
Day 10–18
  ↓ Craft Plutonium Equipment (tools + armor) from Ingots
  ↓ Craft Hazmat Suit from Industrial Fabric (75% radiation resistance)
  ↓
Day 12–20
  ↓ Plant Mutated Seeds on Radioactive Farmland
  ↓ Harvest Healing Petals → craft Radiation Antidotes
  ↓ Craft Radiation Serum (10 min immunity)
  ↓
Day 15–25
  ↓ Craft Nuclear Forge → MK-I upgrades (90% success)
  ↓ Progress to MK-IV (40% success, 1000 energy cost)
  ↓
Day 20–35
  ↓ Collect Titan Core from rare zombie drop or specific craft
  ↓ Summon the Plutonium Titan
  ↓
Day 25–40
  ↓ Kill the Titan (solo: very hard; group: recommended)
  ↓ Collect Titan Fragments, Reactor Heart, Ancient Blueprint
  ↓
Day 35–50
  ↓ Craft Titan Reactor Forge using blueprint
  ↓ Craft Titan Armor (100% radiation immunity — full set)
  ↓ Craft Titan Weapons and Tools (endgame tier)
  ↓
ENDGAME
```

## Stage 1 — Zombie Hunter (Day 1–3)

**Goal:** Survive the first night and begin collecting core materials.

**What happens:**
- 10% of natural zombies spawn as Irradiated Zombies (30% during Radiation Night)
- They deal radiation damage on hit (15 pts per hit by default)
- They drop Radioactive Cores, Mutated Seeds, and rarely Irradiated Hearts

**Key tip:** You don't need to seek out irradiated zombies. They will find you. Your first goal is simply to survive and collect drops.

**Milestone unlocked:** First Radioactive Core

**Approximate cost to unlock next stage:** ~20 Radioactive Cores for a Radiation Drill

---

## Stage 2 — Mining (Day 4–7)

**Goal:** Mine Plutonium Ore safely using the Radiation Drill.

**What happens:**
- Plutonium Ore generates at Y: -64 to -20 in small veins (size 3, 1 vein/chunk)
- Mining without a Radiation Drill exposes you to 25 pts per ore break
- Mining with a Radiation Drill reduces radiation exposure by 75% (≈6 pts per break)

**Key resources needed:**
- Radiation Drill (crafted from Radioactive Cores + Diamonds)
- Radiation Antidote or Hazmat Suit for safety

**Drop rates:**
| Fortune Level | Fragments per Block |
|---|---|
| None | 2 |
| Fortune I | 3 |
| Fortune II | 4 |
| Fortune III | 5 |

**Milestone unlocked:** First Plutonium Ore mined

---

## Stage 3 — Nuclear Smelter (Day 7–14)

**Goal:** Refine Raw Plutonium Fragments into Refined Plutonium Ingots.

**What happens:**
- Place the Nuclear Smelter (crafted from Cores + Iron + Nether materials)
- Load Fragments in the top slot, fuel in the bottom
- Processing: 200 ticks (10 seconds) per fragment
- Warning: Active smelters leak 0.5 rad/tick to nearby players

**Fuel efficiency:**
| Fuel | Ticks |
|---|---|
| Coal | 800 (4 operations) |
| Blaze Rod | 1200 (6 operations) |
| Lava Bucket | 2000 (10 operations) |

**Milestone unlocked:** First Nuclear Smelter crafted

---

## Stage 4 — Plutonium Equipment (Day 10–18)

**Goal:** Craft Plutonium tools, armor, and a Hazmat Suit.

**Equipment tiers:**

| Set | Radiation Resistance | Primary Role |
|---|---|---|
| Hazmat Suit | 75% | Safe ore mining, machine work |
| Plutonium Armor | 50% | Combat, PvP |

**Plutonium tools** mine faster than diamond and apply radiation on hit.

**Critical recommendation:** Always have Hazmat gear before going near the Titan. Without it, the Titan's aura (8-block range, 10 rad/tick) will kill you in under 2 minutes.

**Milestone unlocked:** First Plutonium armor piece crafted

---

## Stage 5 — Radioactive Farming (Day 12–20)

**Goal:** Establish a Mutated Healing Plant farm for sustainable cure production.

**What happens:**
- Plant Mutated Seeds on Radioactive Farmland (crafted or converted farmland)
- Crops grow faster than normal but spread Toxic Blooms to adjacent soil
- Harvest Healing Petals → craft Radiation Antidotes (8 petals each)
- Craft 4 Antidotes into 1 Radiation Serum (10 min full immunity)

**Warning:** Radioactive Farmland emits 1 rad/tick to players standing on it. Always wear Hazmat gear when farming.

**Economy note:** At Stage 5, you should have a stable Antidote supply. Never fight the Titan without at least 5 Antidotes or 1 Serum.

**Milestone unlocked:** First active Radioactive Farm

---

## Stage 6 — Nuclear Forge & Upgrades (Day 15–25)

**Goal:** Upgrade Plutonium equipment through MK-I to MK-IV tiers.

**Upgrade costs:**
| Tier | Energy | Success % | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| MK-I | 100 | 90% | ~1.1 cores per success |
| MK-II | 250 | 75% | ~1.3 cores per success |
| MK-III | 500 | 60% | ~1.7 cores per success |
| MK-IV | 1000 | 40% | ~2.5 cores per success |

Energy is supplied by Radioactive Cores in the forge fuel slot.

**Recommendation:** Upgrade in order. Don't attempt MK-IV without at least 3 backup cores per attempt.

**Milestone unlocked:** First MK-I upgrade completed

---

## Stage 7 — The Titan (Day 20–40)

**Goal:** Summon and defeat the Plutonium Titan.

**Requirements:**
- Titan Core (right-click to summon)
- Full Hazmat or Plutonium armor
- Multiple Radiation Serums
- At least MK-II equipment recommended

**Boss overview:**
| Phase | Health % | Abilities |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | 100–75% | Basic attacks, slow radiation aura |
| 2 | 75–50% | Radiation aura fully active (8-block, 10/tick) |
| 3 | 50–25% | Summons Alpha Zombies (up to 6), Radiation Beam |
| 4 | 25–0% | Full ability rotation, increased damage |

**Health scaling:** The Titan gains 100 HP per player beyond the first (capped at +900 HP for 10 players). A solo fight is 800 HP; a 5-player group faces 1,200 HP.

**Guaranteed drops:** Titan Core, Reactor Heart, 8–16 Titan Fragments

**Cooldown:** 60 minutes before the Titan can respawn (per player).

**Milestone unlocked:** First Titan killed

---

## Stage 8 — Titan Technology (Day 35–50)

**Goal:** Craft the Titan Reactor Forge and produce endgame equipment.

**Requirements:**
- Ancient Reactor Blueprint (70% drop from Titan)
- Reactor Heart (guaranteed Titan drop)
- Titan Fragments (8–16 per kill)

**Titan Armor** grants 100% radiation immunity with the full set bonus. This is the only complete protection against the Titan's aura.

**Titan Weapons** deal AOE radiation damage and apply heavy infection on hit.

**This is the endgame.** At this point, the plugin's progression is complete. Titan-equipped players are the server's elite.

**Milestone unlocked:** First Titan equipment piece crafted

## Rush Detection

The admin console will warn if a player reaches a milestone faster than 50% of the expected minimum time. If you see these warnings, the player may:
- Have found an exploit (check `/nc debug all` output)
- Have received items from another player
- Simply be extremely efficient (acceptable — check context)

Run `/nc debug economy` to check boss cooldowns and machine counts for that player.

## Multiplayer Progression Notes

**Shared resource pools:** Plutonium Ore is a limited server resource. On large servers (50+), increase `ore.veins-per-chunk` to 2–3 in `balance.yml`.

**Titan kill contention:** The Titan drops loot to all significant contributors. Grief-killing (other players killing the Titan before the summoner can benefit) is limited by the contribution system — minimum 5% damage is required to receive any drops.

**Boss cooldown per player:** The 60-minute boss cooldown is per-player, not server-wide. A second player can immediately summon a new Titan without affecting another player's cooldown state.
