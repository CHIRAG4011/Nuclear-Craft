# NuclearCraft: Plutonium Age — Player Guide
Version 1.0.0

Welcome to NuclearCraft. This guide explains the core mechanics every player needs to survive.

---

## The Radiation System

Radiation is measured on a scale from **0 to 1000**. As it rises, you suffer increasingly severe effects.

| Stage | Threshold | Effects |
|---|---|---|
| Safe | 0–99 | No effects |
| Stage 1 | 100–299 | Nausea, slight weakness |
| Stage 2 | 300–499 | Hunger, slowness |
| Stage 3 | 500–749 | Poison, wither |
| Stage 4 | 750–949 | Severe debuffs, blindness |
| Stage 5 | 950–1000 | Critical — certain death |

Radiation **decays naturally** over time when you're not in a radioactive area.

### Sources of Radiation
- Mining **Plutonium Ore** without a Radiation Drill
- Standing near active **Nuclear Smelters** or **Nuclear Forges**
- Being hit by **Irradiated Zombies**
- Walking on **Radioactive Farmland**
- Being near a **Plutonium Titan**
- **Contagion** — radiation spreads to nearby unprotected players

---

## Curing Radiation

### Radiation Antidote
- Crafted from **Healing Petals** and Honey
- Right-click to consume
- Clears all radiation and infection
- Does **not** grant immunity

### Radiation Serum
- Advanced cure requiring Antidotes to craft
- Instantly clears radiation
- Grants **10 minutes of full immunity**
- Rare and expensive

---

## Plutonium Ore

Plutonium Ore spawns deep underground (Y: -64 to -20) in small veins.

**Mining with bare hands or standard tools:** Causes a radiation burst.

**Mining with a Radiation Drill:** Safe — 75% radiation reduction.

---

## The Radiation Drill

The Radiation Drill is required for safe plutonium mining. It mines at diamond+ speed and dramatically reduces radiation exposure. Craft it using Radioactive Cores and Diamonds.

---

## Nuclear Smelter

Place a Nuclear Smelter and right-click to open the interface. Insert:
- **Raw Plutonium Fragments** (top slot)
- **Fuel** — Coal, Blaze Rods, or Lava Buckets (bottom slot)

The machine refines fragments into **Refined Plutonium Ingots** over ~10 seconds.

⚠️ Standing next to a running smelter exposes you to radiation.

---

## Plutonium Equipment

Crafted from Refined Plutonium Ingots. Includes full tool and armor sets:

- **Plutonium Tools** — faster than diamond, deal bonus radiation damage
- **Plutonium Armor** — moderate radiation resistance (50%)
- **Hazmat Suit** — high radiation resistance (75%) — essential for early survival

---

## Nuclear Forge

The Nuclear Forge upgrades your Plutonium and Hazmat equipment through four tiers:

| Tier | Energy Cost | Success Chance |
|---|---|---|
| MK-I | 100 | 90% |
| MK-II | 250 | 75% |
| MK-III | 500 | 60% |
| MK-IV | 1000 | 40% |

Energy is supplied by **Radioactive Cores** in the forge's fuel slot.

---

## Farming

Plant **Mutated Seeds** on **Radioactive Farmland** to grow irradiated crops:

- **Mutated Healing Plants** — harvest **Healing Petals** for radiation cures
- Crops grow faster than normal but contaminate nearby soil
- **Toxic Blooms** can spread if left unchecked

---

## Combat

- Plutonium weapons deal bonus damage based on your enemy's radiation level
- Weapon hits can **infect** targets, spreading radiation on each strike
- Build **Weapon Mastery** by defeating enemies — max level 10

---

## Irradiated Zombies

- Spawn naturally at night (10% chance)
- Deal radiation damage on hit
- Drop **Irradiated Hearts** and **Raw Plutonium Fragments**
- **Alpha Zombies** are larger, brighter, more dangerous

During **Radiation Night**, spawn rates triple. Watch out.

---

## The Plutonium Titan (Boss)

The Titan is summoned using a **Titan Core**. It has four phases:
1. Phase 1 (100-75% HP) — Basic attacks
2. Phase 2 (75-50% HP) — Radiation aura activated
3. Phase 3 (50-25% HP) — Zombie summons + beam attack
4. Phase 4 (<25% HP) — Full ability rotation, enraged

**Drops:** Titan Fragments, Reactor Heart, Ancient Reactor Blueprint

---

## Titan Technology (Endgame)

After defeating the Titan, craft the **Titan Reactor Forge** to access endgame content:

- **Titan Armor** — total radiation immunity (full set), special effects per piece
- **Titan Weapons** — AOE radiation blasts, shockwaves, glowing effects
- **Titan Tools** — maximum speed mining and excavation

This is the pinnacle of NuclearCraft progression.

---

## Quick Tips

- Always carry an Antidote when working near machines or ore
- Hazmat armor is your first priority for safe ore mining
- Radiation Night happens every night cycle — prepare defenses
- The Titan requires a full team — don't attempt it alone
- Check your radiation status with `/nc radiation check`
